About the Site

I spend quite a bit of time on the piano coming up with medleys. It's a 2-pronged process for me. The first way is by using coding. I have a csv file containing a select number of songs from my extended playlist. By using some urllib and shady manipulation, I was able to get the various chord progressions of different songs. Sorting by BPM, I have a coherent flow of songs to follow in the chord-centric medleys. My second method is the accidental method, for I often go on 'transcribing sprees', where I pick out any random song and start playing it by ear. Eventually, this led me to smoothly transition into other songs.
