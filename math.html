About the Site

Math has always been my strong suit. Taking Further Maths in JC and earning multiple awards in Math competitions, I like maths. A sizeable portion of my time in 2017-2018 was spent playing around with graphs on the Desmos app, a tool I often use today, designing graphs and such.

Techniques

I've been using a vast array of new skills and techniques to output better-looking artwork. From specific formulas to in-app shortcuts, here are some of the tips and tricks I have to offer for graphing, primarily Desmos.

Art

I've been on Desmos since 2017, creating simple art works back then. Crafting simpler formulas to perform various art tasks like colouring, shading and cutting. All that culminated to my obtaining of the Gold Award for the Texas Instruments Graphing Competiton in 2018. After that, in 2019, I began posting my artwork to my Instagram, utilising more intensive formulae and concepts to design more intricate pieces of artwork.
