Baarle-Hertog/Nassau

Baarle-Hertog/Nassau is one of the last remaining border confusion. Ever since India and Bangladesh have mostly resolved their disputes, Baarle-Hertog/Nassau has tied with a few other places to becoming 2nd-order enclaves, the highest order currently possible. It's just a place with a very interesting and unique history that I definitely would like to spend a night or so there someday.
