About the Site

I grew up with a wide variety of astronomy childrens' encyclopaediae and factbooks. Quite alot of easily digestible information. Then throughout primary and secondary school, this interest lay dormant. In JC, however, I began taking a deep interest in astrophysics and the heavy-duty calculations involved. This was mainly due to my admiration for the Artifexian YouTube channel. Intense and tangible scientific theory that has spurred me to do plenty of my own research.

Youtube

There are many interesting astronomy channels out there, all with great production quality. With no clickbaity titles necessary, the content is always satisfying. By no means is YouTube my only source of information, but these videos definitely spur me to do my own reading!

Personal projects

I like thinking in hypotheticals, conjuring multiple 'what if' scenarios in my head. I am, however, very disappointed in the common answers given to these sorts of questions. They always assume these scenarios HAVE to be applied to the current state of the planet. I feel that it is much more worthwhile to start the universe from scratch, with everything evolving in this new reality. It's better than to see the universe collapse for example.
