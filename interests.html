About the Site

In pursuit of self-improvement, I always find myself picking up new hobbies and interests to pass the time. People often catch me doing random things and asking why. To that, my response has always been 'Why not?'. After all, there is much to learn. From the outer reaches of space, to the inner workings of the quantum world, one can never finish learning. Here are some of the topics that piqued my interest over the years, sharing relevant resources along the way.
