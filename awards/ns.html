This is the time to spam all the things I want to do. All before university starts. Mostly developing and working on my interests.

Singaporean Males are expected to serve the nation for 2 years (with some exceptions). Possessing the awkward body that I have, I am labelled as unfit. My awards didn't stop here though, for I received the 'Best Trainee' Award for the SCDF Admin Support Course.

I must say I am extremely lucky, for I got posted to an office job. But not only that, I'm a programming-intense data analyst assistant. Perfect for getting some work experience. After all, I get to deal with actual data, craft and maintain lengthy codes, all to solve complicated real-life problems within short time-frames. Pretty cool :P

My jobscope is rather wide-ranging, and getting roped into various projects is definitely interesting. But I guess my main role is to optimise ambulance placement. It's fun to brainstorm possible solutions with my branch-mates. But NOT fun to try to actually execute it. I absolutely need more experience in this to even survive in the working world :/

I'd like to call Python my second language, with it being my primary medium of writing code. With a little bit of Ruby thrown in there, I need to be able to fix and manipulate code in due time. Most of the time, although well-labelled, the codes are hard to follow. Should be because the scripts themselves are complex.

During the ample free time that I have, I create / compose music, pick up several languages simultaneously and go for a variety of courses. I also maintain an Instagram account showcasing some of my graphing art and puzzles.
