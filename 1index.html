About the Site

Hello there! My Name Izz Hafeez and I live in Singapore. Besides creating music, mathematical art and puzzles, I have been learning a few languages on the side. In this site, I share some of the things I've done, as well as my interests, hobbies and thoughts over the years.

Each post is color-coded according to category: Awards, Interests, Personal, Music, Math and Puzzles, so that it is easier to find what you're looking for. Be sure to check out my MuseScore, Instagram and YouTube channels!
