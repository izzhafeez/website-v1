About the Site

I've been involved in music for many stages in my life. It has always been an integral part of my soul. I have quite alot of experience playing piano, with a little time spent on guitar as well. Apart from that, I sang in my VS Choir during my secondary school days. But now I'm more involved in music than ever, creating transcriptions, medleys and even original songs!

My Music Journey

My parents enrolled me in piano lessons when I was 5. I don't remember liking my time here.

After completing the course at Yamaha Music School, aged around 7, I started going for private piano lessons. Once or twice a week, I would go down to my teacher's house for lessons. Conveniently, my neighbour happened to be a piano teacher so yeah.. I quite enjoyed my time there, learning to play many diverse songs.

The highlight of this section of my music life was my piano recital at age 9. Ok so I was currently practicing for my Grade 1 Practical Exam and some music teachers, including my own, were having a piano-ish recital. Most of my teacher's students participated. All older than me. All higher grade than me. But she decided to put me in first because I was 'stable'. yAy

I went on with lessons but stopped grade 5 for both practical and theory because my hands were starting to hurt from playing.

Moving in to Secondary School, I wanted to join Concert Band. My parents were both in MB so I wanted to follow in their footsteps. During CCA selection process, we had to give 9 choices for a CCA. I of course put Band as first. I was satisfied with my 9 choices, as I felt that I would enjoy my time there. But what I did not expect was that the school would place me in CHOIR. Apparently they had fewer people or something. I wanted to appeal into Band at first.
